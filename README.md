# 🔥 安燃无恙 / IgnisShield

> **中文**：安燃无恙是一款面向智慧社区、地下停车场、新能源充电站等场景的新一代AI消防预警设备，集安防监控与火情报警于一体，为传统消防安全提供低成本、高可靠的升级方案。
> **English**: IgnisShield is a next-generation AI fire alarm device for smart communities, parking lots, and EV stations. It integrates security monitoring and fire warning into one, offering a low-cost, high-reliability upgrade for traditional fire safety.

---

## 🌟 项目亮点 / Project Highlights
- **场景全覆盖**：专为复杂室内外场景设计，适配住宅小区、地下车库、充电桩等高频风险场所。
  **Full-scenario Coverage**: Designed for complex indoor and outdoor environments, suitable for residential areas, parking lots, and charging stations.

- **低成本易部署**：无需改造现有基础设施，以摄像头+边缘设备即可快速搭建，大幅降低接入门槛。
  **Low Cost & Easy Deployment**: No major infrastructure changes required. Deploy quickly with a camera and edge device, significantly lowering the barrier to entry.

- **全天候稳定运行**：优化的抗干扰设计，支持低光照、雨雾等复杂环境，实现全年无间断监测。
  **24/7 Stable Operation**: Optimized anti-interference design works in low light, rain, and fog, enabling uninterrupted monitoring year-round.

- **开源开放**：遵循MIT协议开源，欢迎开发者与社区一起迭代优化。
  **Open Source**: Released under the MIT License, welcoming developers and the community to iterate together.
